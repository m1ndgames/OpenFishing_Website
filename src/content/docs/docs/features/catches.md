The **catch log** records every fish you land, with as much or as little detail as you like.

## Fields

- **Species** — what you caught.
- **Weight & length** — measurements for the record.
- **GPS location** — where you caught it; nearby [spots](/docs/features/spots) pick it up
  automatically (within 100 m).
- **Catch-&-release** — flag whether the fish was released.
- **Presentation** — how you were fishing it.
- **Linked lure & spot** — connect the catch to a [lure](/docs/features/lures) from your
  library and to a saved spot.

## Bite Index

Every catch stores an automatic **[Bite Index](/docs/features/bite-index)** — a 0–10 weather
score describing how favourable conditions were at the time and place of the catch. The score
is captured as a snapshot on the catch, so it stays accurate even as the weather changes.
