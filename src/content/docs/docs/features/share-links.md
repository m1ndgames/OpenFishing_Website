---
title: Share links
description: Publish read-only public pages for a lure, spot or catch, and revoke them at any time.
---

**Share links** let you publish a single **lure**, **spot** or **catch** as a read-only public
page — handy for showing off a catch or sharing a lure with a friend without giving them access
to your whole instance.

## How it works

- Create a share link for any individual lure, spot or catch.
- The link opens a **read-only** page showing just that item.
- **Revoke** the link at any time to take the page down again.

Only the specific item you share is exposed; the rest of your data stays private.

## Related

- Share links are read-only, similar in spirit to [Demo mode](/docs/demo/demo-mode).
