title: Tackle
description: Track your rods, reels, lines and combos, including line-spool history.
---

The **tackle box** keeps an inventory of your gear so you know exactly what you own and how
it's set up.

## What you can track

- **Rods** — your rod inventory.
- **Reels** — your reels.
- **Lines** — line types and specs, with **line-spool logs** that record when you spooled a
  reel with a given line so you can see its history over time.
- **Combos** — pair a rod and reel (and line) into a named combo you actually fish.
