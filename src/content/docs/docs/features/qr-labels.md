---
title: QR labels
description: Print small QR-code labels for your lures to link a physical lure back to its entry.
---

OpenFishing can generate **QR-code labels** so a physical lure links straight back to its
entry in your library.

## Printing labels

- Labels are sized for **12.5 mm** label stock — small enough to fit on a lure or its box.
- You can print labels for **unlabeled lures** in a batch.
- Each label encodes a link built from your **`BASE_URL`**, so scanning it opens the right
  lure. Make sure `BASE_URL` is set to your real public URL — see
  [Environment variables](/docs/configuration/environment-variables).

## Related

- Labels reference the sequential `#0001` numbers described in [Lures](/docs/features/lures).
