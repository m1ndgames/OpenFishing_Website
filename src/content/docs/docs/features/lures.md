The **lure library** is the heart of OpenFishing — a visual catalogue of every lure you own.

## What you can track

- **Photos** — one or more images per lure, stored on your uploads volume.
- **Tags & species** — categorize lures and note the species they target.
- **Favourites** — flag the lures you reach for most.
- **Lost-lure tracking** — mark a lure as lost so it drops out of your active collection
  without deleting its history.
- **Amount** — how many of a given lure you have.
- **Sequential numbers** — each lure gets an automatic sequential ID (e.g. `#0001`), handy
  for [QR labels](/docs/features/qr-labels).

## Finding lures

- **Fast client-side filtering & search** — filter by tag, species or favourite and search by
  name instantly, without a round-trip to the server.
- **Auto-suggest** — tag and species inputs suggest values you've already used.
- **Pagination** — large collections page smoothly.
